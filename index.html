About

Hi, I'm Roxy. I'm a full stack web developer based in sunny Orlando, Fla. I work as a developer for Yellow Shoes Creative Group– the in-house advertising agency for the Walt Disney Company. Prior, I completed five months of over 600 hours of training on building and deploying full stack web applications using HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, React, Ruby on Rails and Express.

I also have experience integrating third-party API's and working in teams and collaborating on GitHub. Before choosing to to pursue code fulltime, I worked in eCommerce and retail as a buyer for Amazon and Macy's, Inc. In all my decisions, I keep the end goal in mind: the customer. I am open to freelance, contract roles, internships and apprenticeships, as well as fulltime opportunities. I look forward to hearing from you!
